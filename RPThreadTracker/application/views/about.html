About This Website

Hello, there! My name is Rosalind ( blackjackkent in the Tumblrverse), and I am a Tumblr geek, RP addict, and software engineer. This site is designed to help members of the Tumblr roleplay/collaborative writing community to monitor the activity of their threads all in one place, for multiple threads and multiple accounts.

I came up with this idea primarily as a way to combat the strange behavior of the Tumblr activity page, which, while no doubt fine for the average user, is unreliable for the needs of the Tumblr RP community. While still somewhat limited by the capabilities of the Tumblr API and its related systems, I hope this app will be useful to those who participate in Tumblr RP, allowing a better way to track interactions that are currently open and manage threads individually.

If you have any suggestions, find any bugs in the site, or just want to communicate, please feel free to contact me!

Note: This site is in no way affiliated with Tumblr, nor does it make any direct connection to your account or posting permissions. It retrieves information solely through Tumblr's public application programming interface.
